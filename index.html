An Invitation

Help shape what we build together.

Last August, we packed the theater. More than 175 of us came together for the 3rd Annual Community Economic Development Summit. We talked about wealth, identity, and power. We named the audacity it takes to build what our community deserves. The room reached capacity, the conversations went past the closing remarks, and the work didn't stop when we cleared the seats.

This year — Thursday, August 13, 2026 — we come back. Alongside the 5th Annual Black Wall Street Festival, the largest Black-owned festival in Connecticut. With deeper conversations, sharper programming, and a community ready to shape what comes next.

But before we curate the experience, we want to hear from you. Three minutes will help shape the conversations, the programming, and the work we do together this August.
